Search on Google

SynthID is Google's invisible watermarking technology embedded in AI-generated images. It cannot be detected automatically, but you can check manually using Google's reverse image search.

How to check for SynthID:

  1. Click the button above to search on Google.
  2. In the search results, click "About this image".
  3. Look under the image preview for a label that says "Made with Google AI".

If you see "Made with Google AI", the image was generated by a Google AI tool (like Imagen or Gemini) and contains SynthID watermarking.
