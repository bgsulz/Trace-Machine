What is this?
The Image History tool will search the internet for visually similar images to the one being analyzed. You'll get a curated list of sources, as well as an estimated creation date.
This is a powerful tool for distinguishing synthetic and non-synthetic media, as synthetic media was neither visually persusasive nor widespread prior to mid-2022.
Why a donation goal?
