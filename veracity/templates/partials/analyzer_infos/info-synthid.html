SynthID is an invisible watermark embedded in the pixels of some AI-generated or AI-edited images. It is not metadata, unlike C2PA/Content Credentials or EXIF data, so it usually has to be checked with a provider-specific detector.

Google exposes SynthID checks through Google reverse image search and About this image. OpenAI exposes checks through OpenAI Verify. These tools are not interchangeable: Google's tooling may only recognize Google-origin watermarks, while OpenAI Verify reports OpenAI-origin SynthID.

Important limitation: SynthID can be removed or degraded through aggressive editing, resizing, or compression. A negative result from one provider's checker does not rule out another provider's watermark.
